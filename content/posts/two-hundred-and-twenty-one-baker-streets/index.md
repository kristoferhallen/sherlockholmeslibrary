An anthology of short stories that takes Sherlock through time and space. After reading it I am not sure what I have read but I love it. You get everything, a 1930s story featuring a Holmes who runs a traveling carnival, a story with a female Sherlock, one with Sherlock in a world full of Wizards. Definitely not traditional pastiches but if you are ready to challenge your view of who Sherlock and Watson are you should read this book.
