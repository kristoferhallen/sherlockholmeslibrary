When Sherlock Holmes dismisses a desperate young bride who comes to 221B for help, Mrs. Hudson and Mary Watson decide to take matters into their own hands. They investigate on their own, with some help from the Baker Street Irregulars and after a while also Irene Adler who is back in London. 

This is a refreshing take on the Holmes universe, Holmes and Watson are there but not as the main characters, now we get to know Mrs. Hudson and Mary better. The mystery is good and with all the characters you expect including Mycroft and maybe there also is a hint of a criminal mastermind in the shadows.
