A story told from the perspective of Sherlock. After meeting Freud in Vienna in [The Seven-Per-Cent Solution](https://sherlock.kristoferhallen.com/posts/the-seven-per-cent-solution/) Sherlock still needs to keep it a secret that he is alive and in Paris he takes a job as a violinist in an opera house. Someone or something is haunting the opera, is it a phantom? The Woman, Irene Adler, joins the opera and when people are killed Sherlock can't stop himself for going back to his profession as a consulting detective.
