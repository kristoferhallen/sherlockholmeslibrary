The world believes Moriarty died in the Reichenbach falls but he is alive! Moriarty returns from exile and wants revenge on the people destroyed his criminal empire. 
The story is told from the perspective of Moriarty and his gang, a police officer and Sherlock Holmes. Well written with maybe a bit too elaborate plans.
