A collection of Sherlock Holmes short stories by many authors. Most stories are well written in the original style. What makes this a great collection are all the crossovers. Here are stories where Sherlock meets:

* The writer [HG Wells](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/H._G._Wells) and his wife, the author and journalist [Rebecca West](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rebecca_West)
* A monster created by a certain Victor Frankenstein
* Martians
* The gentleman thief [AJ Raffles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A._J._Raffles_(character)). Raffles was created by EW Hornung, the brother-in-law of Sir Arthur Conan Doyle
* [Newbury and Hobbes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/George_Mann_(writer)), steampunk investigator and assistant created by George Mann.
