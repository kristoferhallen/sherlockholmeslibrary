Mary Jekyll, daughter to Dr Henry Jekyll, alone and penniless after her parents' death, starts investigating the secrets of her father's mysterious past. This leads her to Sherlock Holmes and Dr. Watson, and eventually to a group of extraordinary women, some would call them monsters, all created through scientific experimentation. Together they form the Athena Club and set out to solve a series of gruesome murders and try to understand what it is that connects them. 

This is a great mashup, here people from the worlds of Dr Jekyll and Mr Hyde, Rappaccini's Daughter, The Island of Doctor Moreau, Professor Abraham Van Helsing (from Dracula) and Frankenstein meet. It has a special style with the main characters commenting on the story, took some time to get used to.
