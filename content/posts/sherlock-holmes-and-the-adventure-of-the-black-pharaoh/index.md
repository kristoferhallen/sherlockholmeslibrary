When an Egyptologist from the British Museum is found dead with his hands amputated, Holmes and Watson are hired by Lord Silverpin and his sister to find their missing father, Robert Henry, the Fifth Earl of Convarran. The Earl vanished after returning from a disastrous excavation at the Black Pyramid in Dashoor, Egypt, and the Silverpins fear he may have met a similar fate. A well constructed plot, everyone is interested in the artifacts that were found in egypt, including the government through Mycroft and there are hints of a criminal mastermind in the shadows. And are the artifacts even from earth? And who knew what BEACON, the British Empire's Advisory Council on Objects Non-Terrestrial existed?
